Pet Fish

The facts about fish as pets
People like to have fish as pets because for some people fish are calming and they dont make alot of noise unlike cats, dogs or birds.
People also like to have fish because having a fish aquarium could look good in anyones home as a sense of style.
Another fact that peole like about fish, is that they dont need to be cleaned.
Certain types of fish can also interact with their owners and are also playful
